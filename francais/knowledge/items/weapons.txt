::PlasmaPistol
Plasma technology based weapons inflict damage by performing high power thermal discharge. Generating temperatures nearly double that of Sun's surface, these weapons can be devastating. However, they usually have lower fire rates than their laser counterparts due to the longer recharge and cooling times.

Plasma pistol is the most common type of plasma weapon.

::Crossbow
Although not as devastating as firearms or energy weapons, crossbow is still a dangerous device. The bolts it fires can be rigged with explosives or coated with poison, but are otherwise generally less efficient against heavily armored targets. On the other hand, since bolts fly at a relatively low speed they penetrate energy shields more easily.
