::MovementPoints
These points can only be used for movement.

::ActionPoints
These points can be used for performing actions or for movement if there are no movement points left. You can carry over up to {0} unspent action points to the next turn.

::Innervate
Applies the changes made to the psi ability selection. This will instantly drain all of your psi points and reserves.

This can only be done out of combat.
